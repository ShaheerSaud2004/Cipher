A Little About Me

Computer Science student by day, event photographer by weekend. I love capturing the real moments that matter.

Why I Do This

I started photographing MSA events at Rutgers and realized I had a knack for capturing those genuine moments – the laughs, the connections, the community spirit. Now I help organizations tell their real stories, not just polished marketing content.

Being a Computer Science student gives me a different perspective. I understand both the technical side of photography/videography and how to make content that actually works on social media and websites.
